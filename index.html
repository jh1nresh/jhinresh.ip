03 / COMMERCE AGENTWORKING DEMO

SLL-R

Merchant-backed order execution for AI agents.

Binds an exact merchant quote to buyer consent, reserves real capacity, creates one idempotent order, tracks live queue state, and issues a canonical receipt only after fulfillment or customer claim.

  • Quote-bound consent and idempotent execution
  • Payment proof kept separate from fulfillment proof
  • Merchant terminal, MCP, polling and receipt lifecycle

OPERATING METHOD / 05

Build fast. Fail closed.

01

Define the paid job

Start from a costly failure or action—not an impressive model capability.

02

Constrain the action

03

Attack the happy path

04

Leave a receipt

Every meaningful decision becomes inspectable evidence for the next one.
